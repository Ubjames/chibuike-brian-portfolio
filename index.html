The Architect Behind the Screen

I am a software developer with a passion for problem-solving and a deep love for storytelling through code. My journey began with a simple curiosity about how things work, evolving into a career defined by building seamless, high-performance digital experiences.

Today, I focus on merging technical precision with creative vision. I don't just write code; I craft digital environments where users feel understood and engaged.

It all started when I dismantled my first radio at age 10, fascinated by the hidden logic of circuits. That curiosity transitioned into the digital realm during college, where I discovered that lines of code could build worlds.

Over the years, I've worked with startups to scale their MVPs and with established enterprises to modernize legacy systems. My philosophy is simple: technology should be invisible, enabling human intent without friction. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring brutalist architecture or capturing street photography.
